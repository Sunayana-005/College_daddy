Frequently Asked Questions About Generative AI Roadmap

Do I need a PhD to learn Generative AI?

No, you can build powerful applications using existing APIs and tools without deep research knowledge. A strong engineering background helps.

What programming language is best?

Python is the undisputed king of AI development due to its rich ecosystem of libraries like PyTorch, TensorFlow, and LangChain.
