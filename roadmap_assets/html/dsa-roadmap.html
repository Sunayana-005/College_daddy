Frequently Asked Questions About DSA Roadmap

How long does it take to learn Data Structure and Algorithm?

It takes 6 months to 1 years to gain proficiency in DSA, depending on your background. If you have prior programming, you can progress faster. Our roadmap is structured for a 9-12 month learning journey with consistent practice.

What programming languages should I learn for DSA?

Python is best for beginners due to simple syntax.C++ or Java are also popular choices for interviews and competitive coding.

Do I need advanced math for DSA?

No, You only need basic mathematics and logical thinking.

How many hours should I practice daily?

12 hours/day consistently is enough to see good progress.

Do I need to memorize algorithms?

No - focus on understanding logic and patterns, not memorization

Where to practice DSA?

GeeksforGeeks Practice(sorted by topic): https://practice.geeksforgeeks.org/

LeetCode(popular): https://leetcode.com/
